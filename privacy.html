Privacy Policy for Pin Ally

Effective date: April 7, 2026

Pin Ally is designed to help you save and organize places that matter to you. We respect your privacy. Pin Ally does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, marketers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

Information We Collect

Pin Ally does not collect personal information from you for marketing, advertising, or data-sharing purposes.

Any pins, notes, photos, tags, or related content you create in the app are controlled by you.

How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used only to provide the app’s core features, such as saving, organizing, viewing, and managing your pins and related details.

Sharing

Pin Ally does not automatically share your data with anyone. If you choose to share a pin or export content, that action is initiated by you, and you decide who receives it.

Third-Party Access

Pin Ally does not provide your data to third-party partners for marketing, advertising, profiling, or similar purposes.

Your Control

You are in control of your data. You may:

  • Create, edit, and delete your pins and related information at any time.
  • Choose whether to share any pin with someone else.
  • Decide what information to include in your pins, notes, and photos.

Data Security

We aim to keep Pin Ally simple and privacy-focused. Any information stored by the app is meant to support your personal use of the app’s features.

Children’s Privacy

Pin Ally is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the support page.

In short: Pin Ally does not collect or share your data for marketing or third-party use. You control your content, and sharing only happens when you choose it.
